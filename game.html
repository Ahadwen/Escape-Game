How to play

Core rules for survival, loot, and the card system. Use Back when you are ready to pick a hero.

  • Hit points & health — you have a life total shown on the HUD. Enemy hits and hazards chip it down; at 0 HP the run ends. Some effects grant temporary extra protection.
  • Health crystals — green pickups on the map restore HP (and slightly refresh your other ability cooldowns) so you can recover after a bad trade.
  • Ultimate (R) — comes from the Ace in your rank deck: each Ace has a random suit and a random ultimate (orbiting shields, push waves, timelock, or vitality). Press R when it is ready (20s cooldown after each use). After you die, R opens character select so you can pick a hero again and start a fresh run.
  • Items (suit cards) — floating cards are gear. Touch one to open the pickup flow: your deck has one slot per rank (A through K); you also have three backpack packs. Place a pickup in its rank slot (or swap with the card already there), stash it in the backpack, or leave it. Only passives from cards in your rank deck apply; the backpack is for swaps and planning.
  • Set bonuses — collecting seven cards of the same suit in your rank deck unlocks an extra bonus for that suit (shown under your deck during a run). Diamonds can offer a one-time choice for the Knight; Hearts, Clubs, and Spades each have their own payoff, and some bonuses read differently on the Hungry Rogue.
